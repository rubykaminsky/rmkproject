527 11th Street

This is a situation comedy webcomic about neighbors in an apartment building. Below we have our characters who live in the building! From left to right, Gemma, Penny, Louie, George, and Max.

Gemma has been living in the building her whole life, she loves EDM, vaccuums regularly, and is keen to get to know *some* of her new neighbors. If you engage her in the elevator, she will start complaining about puppy that has moved in upstairs.

Penny moved in to the building just weeks ago. Many people mistake her for the DJ type, but really she just has a sticky fingers problem...

Louie is the building's French resident. He comes from the Bourgogne region. His mother often sends him cheese. This cheese's stench somehow, but very often, infiltrates the entire building.

George and Max have just gotten a puppy. They were early on the gentrifying scene, and now like to talk about how much the neighborhood has changed since they moved in. Gemma scoffs.
